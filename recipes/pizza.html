Pizza

Pizza is a globally popular Italian dish originating from Naples, consisting of a flattened, leavened wheat dough base topped with savory ingredients, most commonly tomato sauce and mozzarella cheese. Baked at high temperatures, often in wood-fired ovens, it is served hot with various toppings like meats, vegetables, and herbs.
