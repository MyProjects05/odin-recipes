Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian baked dish featuring alternating layers of wide, flat pasta sheets, savory sauces (typically meat-based ragù or béchamel), and cheeses like ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan. It is assembled, topped with cheese, and baked al forno (in the oven) until bubbling and golden.
