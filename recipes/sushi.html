Sushi

Sushi is a traditional Japanese dish defined by vinegared, seasoned rice (shari or sumeshi) combined with various ingredients, most commonly raw or cooked seafood, vegetables, and nori (seaweed). Originating as a method for preserving fish, it has evolved into a diverse culinary art form featuring various styles like nigiri, maki, and temaki.
