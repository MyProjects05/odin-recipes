Burger

A burger is a popular, hot sandwich consisting of a cooked, often grilled, ground meat patty (classically beef) served inside a sliced bread roll or bun. It is commonly customized with toppings like lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, cheese, and condiments such as ketchup, mustard, or mayonnaise for a savory, textured meal.
